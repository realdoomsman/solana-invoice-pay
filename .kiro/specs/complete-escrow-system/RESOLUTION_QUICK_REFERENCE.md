## ðŸ“Š Common Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Seller Ghosted
**Evidence**: No communication, no work submitted
**Decision**: Refund to Buyer (100%)
**Notes**: "Seller failed to communicate or deliver any work. Full refund to buyer."

### Scenario 2: Partial Work Done
**Evidence**: 3 of 5 milestones completed
**Decision**: Partial Split (40% buyer, 60% seller)
**Notes**: "Seller completed 60% of work. Splitting funds accordingly."

### Scenario 3: Quality Dispute
**Evidence**: Work delivered but quality issues
**Decision**: Partial Split (60% buyer, 40% seller)
**Notes**: "Work delivered but below expected quality. Buyer receives majority, seller compensated for effort."

### Scenario 4: Buyer Unreasonable
**Evidence**: All deliverables met, buyer wants more
**Decision**: Release to Seller (100%)
**Notes**: "Seller fulfilled all agreed deliverables. Buyer's additional requests were not in original scope."
